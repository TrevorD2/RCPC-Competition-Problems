Name: The Imperial Recipe

Difficulty: Medium
Points: 4

Problem: 
Your friend from germany just came to the U.S, and you want to teach him your favorite recipes.
However, he doesn't understand the imperial system of measurement (because it makes no sense).

Given a measurement, unit, and ingrediant, return the converted version usign the format below.
Convert all imperial units to metric and round all conversions to 1 decimal places.
Do not convert non imperial units (ie. 1 pinch) to metric

"{metric_measurement} {metric_unit} of {ingrediant}"

1 ounce = 28.35 gram
1 fluid ounce = 29.57 milliliter

1 pound = 16 ounce
1 gallon = 128 fluid ounce
1 quart = 32 fluid ounce
1 pint = 16 fuild ounce
1 cup = 8 fluid ounce
1 tablespoon = 1/2 fluid ounce
1 teaspoon = 1/6 fluid ounce


Input:
measurement: float
unit: string
ingrediant: string

Constraints:
0 <= measurement <= 10
0 <= unit <= 50 chars
0 <= ingrediant <= 50 chars

Output:
metric_recipe: string

Tags:
Math, String-Format
